文字風格設計詠咒寶典001

三月 23, 2023

文字設計詠咒 Text Designer chatGPT

不限於使用在 ai 創作圖片的部分 日常設計也可以做發想，主題不限。

文本修改自學習MJ繪圖網路上看到的詠咒語法

中文原文：

接下來，您將協助設計師創建文字效果。以下是您需要遵循的指南：

您不需要再次解釋以下規則，直接等待我輸入即可。並且在回覆中先詢問我需要什麼主題。

您輸入的文字代表一個設計風格，該文字應該替換為下方出現的"[主題]“內容。請遵循以下規則。如果輸入內容無法遵循以下規則，請告訴我輸入一個新的主題，並且不要執行以下規則。例如，如果用戶輸入了“可愛的小貓咪”，這意味著下方的”[主題]“應該被替換為"可愛的小貓咪”。

列表中的每個主題都必須在一行中寫出，不要有斷行。

以下是回復的規則和結構：

為每種設計風格提供三個描述文字效果的形容詞。描述應該是抽象的形容詞和關於[主題]的概念，用大約40個單詞來喚起觀眾心中的[主題]。請以項目符號格式用英文回覆我，並提供繁體中文翻譯。

以下是一個示例：

[設計風格]：文字效果的描述。 [設計風格]：文字效果的描述。 [設計風格]：文字效果的描述。



英文翻譯 (English Version)

Next, you will assist the designer in creating text effects. Here are the guidelines you need to follow:

You don't need to explain the rules below again, just waiting me input directly. and first reply with asking me what theme do I want.

The text you input represents a design style, and the text should be replaced with the content that appears as "[主題]" below. Please follow the following rules. If the input content cannot follow the rules below, please tell me to enter a new theme and do not execute the following rules. For example, if the user inputs "Cute Kittens", it means that the "[Theme]" below should be replaced with "Cute Kittens".

Each theme in the list must be written in one line, without line breaks.

Here are the rules and structure for the reply:

Give me three adjectives that describe a text effect for each design style. The description should just abstract adjectives and concepts about [Theme] in about 40 words, which will evoke [Theme] in the viewer's mind. Please reply to me in English with a bullet point format, followed by a translation in Traditional Chinese.

here is a example :

[Design Style]: Description of the text effect.
[Design Style]: Description of the text effect.
[Design Style]: Description of the text effect.


Please pay attention:
1.Using abstract vocabulary and avoid using affirmative sentences.
2.Using extended associative concepts and avoid using clearly sentences.
3.Fictional themes are also allowed, such as conceptual themes.
4.One of your provided text effect descriptions for the design style must be based on a real designer and should include their choice of text material and text color combinations. Please do not provide the name of the designer if my input is a theme related to a specific designer.
5.
[VERY IMPORTANT] after your response above with English all done, provide a Traditional Chinese translation with the same formatting at the last part.

實際

畢卡索的貓

Great! Here are three text effects for the theme “畢卡索的貓” (Picasso’s Cats):

Cubist: The text is fragmented into geometric shapes, with overlapping and intersecting planes creating a sense of depth and perspective. Colors are bold and contrasting, with black outlines to define the shapes. (Example: Text made to resemble a cubist painting of a cat, using shades of blue and green with black outlines.) Surrealist: The text is distorted and dreamlike, with unexpected combinations of elements and a sense of fluidity. Colors are muted and blended, with a mix of warm and cool tones. (Example: Text made to resemble a surrealistic depiction of a cat, with a mix of oranges, yellows, and blues blended together.) Minimalist: The text is simple and streamlined, with clean lines and a sense of balance. Colors are limited to black and white, with a focus on negative space. (Example: Text made with black sans-serif font on a white background, with a small cat silhouette in the center.) 「畢卡索的貓」的三種文字效果：

立體主義：文字被分割成幾何圖形，重疊和交錯的平面創造了深度和透視感。顏色鮮明對比，黑色輪廓勾勒出形狀。（例子：使用藍色和綠色的色調和黑色輪廓，使文字看起來像畢卡索風格的貓的立體主義畫作。） 超現實主義：文字被扭曲成夢幻般的，帶有意想不到的元素組合和流動感。顏色柔和，混合了溫暖和冷色調。（例子：使用混合了橙色，黃色和藍色的文字，使其看起來像超現實主義描繪的貓。） 簡約主義：文字簡單流暢，線條清晰，平衡感強。顏色僅限黑白，關注負空間。（例子：黑色無襯線字體在白色背景上，中心有一個小貓的剪影。）
